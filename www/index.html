About me

I am a Software Analyst and Developer with a scientific and technological background. My expertise are the technologies behind Geographic Information Systems (GIS), however I am open to work on other fields of Software Engineering where I can express my creativeness, such as Web Portals where I am gaining more experience recently.

My career started in 2006 in United Kingdom, where I had the chance to work as Data Analyst in international environments. Two years and a half later, I moved back to Spain where I have focused more on Software Analyst and Development.

Regarding projects, I have worked for a wide range of sectors such as Transport, Civil Engineering, Environment, Telecom, Energy and Territorial Planning. From all them, I would like to highlight The Olympics 2012, iCOM (Software Suite for Aeronautical operations), Jeddah Strategic Development Plan, Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI) for the Cuenca Municipality and the Transport Network Design for the Beijing Financial District.

As Software Analyst and Developer I feel comfortable with JAVA, JSP, JSF, PL/PgSQL, HTML5 and JavaScript. While as Data Analyst I am familiarized with studies of Spatial/3D and Networks. I also would like to mention that I am used to work under Agile Methodologies (SCRUM) and I am follower of Open Spatial Consortium (OGC) standards and OSGeo projects.
